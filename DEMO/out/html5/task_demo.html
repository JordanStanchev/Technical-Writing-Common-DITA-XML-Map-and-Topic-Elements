How to Start the Calculator App

Here you will find information on how to start the Caclulator app on your mobile phone.

To use the calculator app, you need to unlock your phone first.

You can use a calculator app on your smartphone. You will find it useful, when you need to quickly calculate some numbers.

  1. Navigate to the Calculator application icon.
  2. Tap the icon.

The application starts. You are now ready to calculate with it.

For example, if you have purchased a book for 5$ and you have given 10$, how much change should you expect? Answer is 10-5=5 $ change.
